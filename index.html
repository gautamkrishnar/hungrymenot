About Hungry Me Not

Hungrymenot is the ultimate eat-out guide. At hungrymenot our vision is to make food exploration a lot easier for foodies. We not only allow you to explore restaurants around you but find food as well. Hungrymenot application was developed keeping foodies in mind. We have taken great care to identify your eat-out problems and tried to solve them. Hungrymenot is not a restaurant directory application, and certainly not a scanned menu listing.

We have introduced many innovative features in our application

  • Locate and browse through food around you.
  • Curated menus, don't zoom in those scanned menus ever again!
  • We will bring you the latest offers from the restaurants near you.
  • We provide every detail for you to make an informed choice on where to eat out.
